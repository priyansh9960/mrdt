About the School:

Starlight Academy, established in 2005, is a center of academic brilliance and holistic education. The institution stands tall with a mission to cultivate curious minds, responsible citizens, and confident individuals. With modern infrastructure and value-based learning, it shapes students into the leaders of tomorrow.

Location:

123 Future Street, Learning City, State - 456789

Affiliation & Curriculum:

Affiliated with CBSE, New Delhi, the school offers a well-rounded curriculum from Nursery to Class XII. The academic structure emphasizes both theoretical knowledge and practical application.

Facilities:

Science Laboratories, Computer Lab, Smart Classrooms, Sports Facilities, Well-stocked Library, Activity Rooms for Music and Arts, and Career Counseling sessions for higher classes.

Vision & Mission:

To create a nurturing environment where students can grow intellectually, emotionally, and socially. The school's vision revolves around innovation, inclusion, and inspiration.
