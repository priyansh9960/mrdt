Admission Process

Step 1: Application

Fill out the online application form or collect it from the school office

Step 2: Document Submission

Submit required documents including previous academic records

Step 3: Admission

Fee payment and final admission confirmation

Required Documents

For Primary Classes (1-5)

  • Birth Certificate
  • Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • Report Card of Previous Class
  • Passport Size Photographs
  • Aadhar Card Copy
  • Parent's ID Proof

For Middle School (6-8)

  • All Documents Required for Primary
  • Previous 2 Years' Report Cards
  • Character Certificate

For Secondary (9-10)

  • All Documents Required for Middle School
  • Migration Certificate (if applicable)
